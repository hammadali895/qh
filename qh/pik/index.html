About QH Cottage

Cottage House is a registered and professional trading company based in Sialkot, Pakistan. We are approved by the Sialkot Chamber of Commerce & Industry (SCCI) and operate in both local and international markets. Our core business is focused on leather products and accessories, and we aim to deliver high-quality items with modern designs and excellent durability.

We offer a wide range of products including leather jackets, gloves, belts, caps, and leather care kits. Each product is carefully selected and tested to ensure it meets international standards. We work with experienced manufacturers and suppliers to maintain consistency in quality and timely delivery.

Our mission is to provide premium leather goods that combine style, comfort, and affordability. We are committed to customer satisfaction and strive to build long-term relationships with our clients. Whether you are a retailer, wholesaler, or individual customer, QH Cottage is your trusted partner for high-quality leather products.
